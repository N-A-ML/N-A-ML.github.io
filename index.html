About Chopper

Chopper about to administer medicine

  1. Chopper ate the human-human fruit and gained human-like characteristics
  2. He learnt medicine from his mentors: Kureha and Hiriluk
  3. He joined the strawhat crew to travel the world with his friends and learn about medicine
  4. Chopper can use rumble balls to enhance his abilities in various ways
